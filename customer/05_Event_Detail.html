About the Festival

The Qatar Cultural Festival 2025 is the country's premier cultural celebration, bringing together the finest elements of Qatari heritage, arts, and traditions. This immersive four-day event transforms Katara Cultural Village into a vibrant showcase of Qatar's rich cultural tapestry.

Whether you're a local resident, expatriate, or visitor, this festival offers a unique opportunity to experience the authentic spirit of Qatar through interactive exhibitions, live performances, and cultural exchanges.

What to Expect

The Qatar Cultural Festival offers a vibrant celebration of Qatari heritage through music, dance, food, and art. Visitors can enjoy traditional performances, taste authentic cuisine, shop for handcrafted items, and participate in cultural workshops.

This year's festival features special performances by renowned Qatari musicians, a dedicated children's area with traditional games, and an expanded food court showcasing regional specialties from across the country.

Additional Information

Age Restrictions

Suitable for all ages. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.

Accessibility

Venue is wheelchair accessible with designated viewing areas.

Photography

Photography is permitted for personal use only. No professional equipment without prior authorization.

Dress Code

Please dress modestly out of respect for local culture. Shoulders and knees should be covered. Light, breathable clothing recommended due to evening heat.

Arrival & Entry

Arrive 30 minutes before performances. Entry closes 15 minutes after show times. Bring your ticket and valid ID for verification.

What to Bring

Water bottle (refill stations available), comfortable walking shoes, sun protection for daytime activities, and cash for artisan market purchases.

Not Permitted

Outside food and beverages, professional cameras without permits, drones, pets (except service animals), and large bags or backpacks.
